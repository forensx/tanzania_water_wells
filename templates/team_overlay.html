Meet the Team

JCIB ForensX
The JCIB ForensX team was founded in 2017 as a school team housed at the Jefferson County International Baccalaureate (JCIB) school. After recognizing a lack of Computer Science-focused extracurricular activities at the JCIB program, the team formed with the directive of increasing Computer Science engagement within the student body. The ForensX team looks to leverage emerging technology for the positive development of our community. We developed our app, Tanzania: Water Well Analytics to bring awareness to the global issue of water security.
